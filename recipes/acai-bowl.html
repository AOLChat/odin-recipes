Acai Bowl

Description

This bowl is a sweet but healthy treat any time of day!

Original recipe by Tye Taylor on allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 cup acai berry sorbet
  • 2 tablespoons granola, or as desired
  • 4 strawberries, sliced
  • 1 banana
  • 2 teaspoons unsweetened coconut flakes, or as desired
  • 1 teaspoon honey, or as desired

Steps

  1. Place acai sorbet in a bowl
  2. Top with layer of granola
  3. Line strawberries and bananas on granola layer
  4. Finish with coconut flakes and a drizzle of honey
