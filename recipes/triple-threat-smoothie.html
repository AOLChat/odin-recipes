Triple Threat Smoothie

Description

This berry blend smoothie is the perfect drink under the hot sun!

Original recipe by HEJEANS on allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 kiwi, sliced
  • 1 banana, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup blueberries
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 (8 ounce) container peach yogurt

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to blender
  2. Blend until smooth
